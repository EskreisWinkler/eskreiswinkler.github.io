Coursework
  • Measure Theoretic Probability (STAT381, STAT383): Theoretical introduction to probability measures, random variables, integration, convergence, conditional expectation and martingales.
  • Mathematical Statistics (STAT304, STAT301, STAT302): Distribution theory, quadratic forms, data reduction and Bayesian decision theory.
  • Computational Statistics (STAT309, STAT310, STAT377): Numerical linear algebra, convex analysis, convex optimization, machine learning from a probabilistic approach.
  • Nonparametric Estimation (STAT374): Bias-variance tradeoff, local linear regression, approaches to density estimation, minimax theory, nonparametric Bayes.
  • Stochastic Simulation (STAT315): Approaches to simulation with an introduction to topics ranging from random number sampling to importance and sequential importance sampling to MCMC simulation.
  • Topics in Statistical Machine Learning (STAT377-90): Introduction to Representation Theory and contemporary applications. Connections between representation theory and multiresolution analysis.
